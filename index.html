Places Must Visit

Church

was established in 1978

This church is a beautiful and historic building with stunning architecture and intricate details. It had a rich history and is a place of worship for many people. Despite its age, it still holds a special place in the hearts of those who visited it, and it is a testament to the enduring power of faith and community.



Library

was established in 1980

The school library is a quiet and peaceful place, filled with books, computers, and other resources for students to use. It was a great place to study, read, or work on projects, and it provides a welcoming environment for students to learn and grow. The library is an essential part of the school, and it plays an important role in shaping the minds of young people.



Auditorium

was established in 1982

The auditorium is a large and impressive space with comfortable seating and excellent acoustics. It is often used for school assemblies, concerts, and other events, and it can accommodate a large number of people. The stage is well-lit and equipped with state-of-the-art technology, making it the perfect venue for performances of all kinds. The auditorium is a hub of activity and creativity, and it is a vital part of the school community.
